Deep Breathing Exercise Tips

  • Find a Comfortable Position
    • Sit or lie down in a relaxed position.
    • Keep your spine straight but not rigid.
    • Rest your hands on your lap or stomach.
  • Focus on Your Breath
    • Close your eyes to eliminate distractions.
    • Inhale deeply through your nose, filling your belly with air.
    • Exhale slowly through your mouth or nose.
  • Use a Breathing Pattern
    • 4-7-8 Technique
      • Inhale for 4 seconds.
      • Hold your breath for 7 seconds.
      • Exhale for 8 seconds.
    • Box Breathing
      • Inhale for 4 seconds.
      • Hold for 4 seconds.
      • Exhale for 4 seconds.
      • Hold for 4 seconds again.
  • Engage Your Diaphragm
    • Place one hand on your chest and the other on your abdomen.
    • Ensure your abdomen rises more than your chest.
    • Breathe slowly and steadily.
