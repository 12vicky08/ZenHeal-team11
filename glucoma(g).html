What Is Glaucoma?

Glaucoma is a condition that damages your eye's optic nerve, and it gets worse over time.

Glaucoma Causes

The fluid inside your eye, called aqueous humor, usually flows out of your eye through a mesh-like channel.

Glaucoma Risk Factors

Glaucoma mostly affects adults older than 40, but young adults, children, and even infants can have it.
