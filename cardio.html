Tips for Regular Cardiovascular Exercise

  • Set a Routine
    • Exercise at the same time daily to form a habit.
    • Start with 20-30 minutes per session and gradually increase.
  • Choose Activities You Enjoy
    • Running, swimming, cycling, or brisk walking.
    • Try dance-based workouts like Zumba for variety.
  • Stay Hydrated
    • Drink water before, during, and after exercise.
    • Avoid sugary or high-caffeine drinks.
  • Warm Up and Cool Down
    • Start with 5-10 minutes of light stretching or walking.
    • End with stretching to prevent muscle stiffness.
  • Monitor Your Progress
    • Use a fitness tracker or app to track your workouts.
    • Set goals like increasing distance or speed gradually.
  • Listen to Your Body
    • Stop if you feel pain, dizziness, or extreme fatigue.
    • Adjust intensity based on your fitness level.
