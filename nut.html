Tips for Maintaining a Nutritious Diet

  • Eat a Balanced Diet
    • Include a variety of fruits and vegetables.
    • Consume whole grains like brown rice and oats.
    • Choose lean proteins such as fish, poultry, and legumes.
  • Stay Hydrated
    • Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.
    • Limit sugary drinks like soda and artificial juices.
  • Control Portion Sizes
    • Use smaller plates to avoid overeating.
    • Eat slowly and listen to your hunger cues.
  • Limit Processed Foods
    • Avoid foods high in sugar, salt, and unhealthy fats.
    • Read nutrition labels before purchasing packaged items.
  • Practice Healthy Snacking
    • Opt for nuts, yogurt, or fresh fruits instead of chips or candy.
    • Keep healthy snacks readily available to curb hunger.
  • Plan Your Meals
    • Prepare home-cooked meals instead of eating out.
    • Plan weekly grocery lists to include nutritious options.
