Daily Meditation Routine

  • Set a Time:
    • Choose a consistent time each day.
    • Early morning or before bed works best.
  • Find a Quiet Space:
    • Pick a distraction-free environment.
    • Dim the lights or use candles.
  • Choose a Meditation Type:
    • Mindfulness meditation.
    • Guided meditation.
    • Breathing exercises.
  • Use a Comfortable Posture:
    • Sit cross-legged or on a chair.
    • Keep your spine straight.
  • Focus on Your Breath:
    • Inhale deeply and exhale slowly.
    • Bring attention back if your mind wanders.
  • Start with Short Durations:
    • Begin with 5-10 minutes.
    • Gradually increase the duration.
  • End with Gratitude:
    • Reflect on positive thoughts.
    • Express gratitude for the moment.
