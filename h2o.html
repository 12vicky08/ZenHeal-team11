Stay Hydrated: Daily Tips

  • Set a Daily Water Goal
    • Aim for at least 8 glasses (2 liters) of water per day.
    • Adjust based on your activity level and climate.
  • Carry a Water Bottle
    • Keep a reusable water bottle with you at all times.
    • Choose a bottle with measurement markings to track intake.
  • Drink Water Before Meals
    • Have a glass of water before eating to aid digestion.
    • It helps control appetite and prevents overeating.
  • Eat Water-Rich Foods
    • Include fruits like watermelon, cucumber, and oranges.
    • Soups and smoothies can also contribute to hydration.
  • Set Reminders
    • Use a phone app or alarm to remind you to drink water.
    • Link drinking water to routine activities (e.g., after brushing teeth).
  • Monitor Urine Color
