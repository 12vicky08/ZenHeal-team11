Brain Health Tips

  • Include Healthy Fats:
    • Consume Omega-3 fatty acids found in fish, flaxseeds, and walnuts.
    • Use healthy oils like olive oil and coconut oil in your diet.
    • Avoid trans fats and excessive saturated fats.
  • Stay Hydrated:
    • Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
    • Reduce consumption of sugary and caffeinated beverages.
  • Exercise Regularly:
    • Engage in physical activities like walking, jogging, or yoga.
    • Exercise increases blood flow to the brain and boosts cognitive function.
  • Get Enough Sleep:
    • Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night.
    • Avoid screens before bedtime to improve sleep quality.
  • Manage Stress:
    • Practice mindfulness, meditation, and deep breathing exercises.
    • Engage in hobbies and activities that bring joy and relaxation.
