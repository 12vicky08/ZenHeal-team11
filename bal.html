Daily Physical Activity Tips

  • Start Your Day with Movement
    • Stretch or do light yoga after waking up.
    • Go for a short walk or jog to energize your body.
  • Incorporate Exercise into Your Routine
    • Take the stairs instead of the elevator.
    • Walk or bike instead of driving for short distances.
  • Stay Active at Work
    • Stand up and stretch every hour.
    • Use a standing desk if possible.
  • Engage in Recreational Activities
    • Play a sport or dance for fun and fitness.
    • Try new activities like swimming or hiking.
  • End Your Day with Relaxing Movement
    • Do some light stretching before bed.
    • Take a slow evening walk to unwind.
