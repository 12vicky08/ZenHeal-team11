Importance of Lung Health

Healthy lungs are vital for proper oxygen supply and overall well-being. Keeping your lungs in good condition can prevent breathing difficulties and chronic illnesses.

Common Respiratory Issues

Several conditions can affect lung health, including:

  • Asthma (inflammation of the airways causing difficulty in breathing)
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
  • Pneumonia (lung infection leading to inflammation)
  • Bronchitis (inflammation of the bronchial tubes)
  • Lung cancer
