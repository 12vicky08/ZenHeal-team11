Tips to Stay Connected with Loved Ones

  • Regular Communication
    • Schedule weekly calls or video chats
    • Use messaging apps for daily check-ins
    • Send voice notes for a personal touch
  • Plan Virtual Activities
    • Watch movies together online
    • Play multiplayer games
    • Cook and share recipes over a video call
  • Celebrate Special Occasions
    • Send digital greeting cards
    • Organize surprise video parties
    • Mail gifts or handwritten letters
  • Stay Active on Social Media
    • Share updates and photos
    • Comment and engage with their posts
    • Join family or friend groups
  • Support Each Other Emotionally
    • Check in on mental well-being
    • Share uplifting messages
    • Offer help in tough times
