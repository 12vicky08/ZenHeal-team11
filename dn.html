Tips for Eating a Balanced Diet with Fruits and Vegetables

  • Eat a Variety of Fruits and Vegetables
    • Include different colors of fruits and vegetables for a range of nutrients.
    • Try seasonal and locally available produce.
  • Make Half Your Plate Fruits and Vegetables
    • Fill at least 50% of your plate with fruits and vegetables at each meal.
    • Incorporate salads, steamed, or grilled veggies.
  • Choose Whole Fruits Over Juices
    • Whole fruits contain more fiber and fewer added sugars.
    • Limit fruit juices to small portions and choose fresh-squeezed or 100% juice.
  • Snack on Fruits and Vegetables
    • Keep pre-cut fruits and vegetables available for easy snacking.
    • Pair veggies with healthy dips like hummus or yogurt.
  • Cook Smartly to Retain Nutrients
    • Use steaming, roasting, or grilling instead of deep frying.
    • Avoid overcooking to preserve vitamins and minerals.
  • Incorporate Fruits and Vegetables into Meals
    • Add vegetables to omelets, soups, and sandwiches.
    • Blend fruits into smoothies or mix them with yogurt.
