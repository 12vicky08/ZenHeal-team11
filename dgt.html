Digital Detox & Screen Time Limiting Tips

  • Set Clear Goals
    • Define why you want a digital detox
    • Set specific screen time limits
  • Use Screen Time Management Tools
    • Enable built-in phone timers
    • Use apps to track usage
  • Create No-Screen Zones
    • Keep devices out of the bedroom
    • Designate tech-free mealtimes
  • Engage in Offline Activities
    • Read books or journals
    • Go for a walk or exercise
  • Establish Tech-Free Hours
    • Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime
    • Take regular breaks during work
  • Reduce Social Media Use
    • Unfollow unnecessary accounts
    • Set time limits for social media apps
  • Use Alternative Entertainment
    • Listen to music or podcasts
    • Practice a hobby like painting or writing
  • Communicate Mindfully
    • Prefer face-to-face interactions
    • Schedule calls instead of texting
