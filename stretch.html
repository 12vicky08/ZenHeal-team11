Practice Stretching and Flexibility Exercises

  • Warm Up First
    • Perform light cardio for 5-10 minutes.
    • Prepare your muscles for stretching.
  • Stretching Techniques
    • Use static stretches (hold for 15-30 seconds).
    • Try dynamic stretches before workouts.
    • Avoid bouncing during stretches.
  • Focus on Major Muscle Groups
    • Stretch legs (quads, hamstrings, calves).
    • Stretch arms and shoulders.
    • Include back and core stretches.
  • Maintain Proper Form
    • Keep your posture aligned.
    • Breathe deeply and relax.
  • Make It a Habit
    • Stretch daily for best results.
    • Incorporate it into your routine.
  • Listen to Your Body
    • Avoid overstretching.
    • Stop if you feel pain.
