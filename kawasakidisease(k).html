What Is Kawasaki Disease?

Kawasaki disease is an illness that causes blood vessels to become inflamed, almost always in young children.

Kawasaki Disease Causes and Risk Factors

The inflammation of Kawasaki disease can damage a child's coronary arteries, which carry blood to their heart.

Kawasaki Disease Symptoms

Kawasaki disease comes on fast, and symptoms show up in phases(High fever,Rash)

Kawasaki disease rash

If your child has Kawasaki disease, you'll probably notice a rash in their genital area or in the area between their chest and legs.

Kawasaki Disease Diagnosis

Your doctor will do a physical exam and ask about your child's symptoms(Red eyes,rash,swollen lymph nodes)

Kawasaki disease and COVID-19

Kids who get COVID-19 can also end up with a condition called multisystem inflammatory syndrome (MIS-C).

Kawasaki Disease Treatment

Your child may have a lot of pain from the fever, swelling, and skin problems. Their doctor might prescribe medicine to make them feel better, such as aspirin and drugs that prevent blood clots.

Kawasaki Disease Complications

Because it involves a child's heart, this illness can be scary( (dysrhythmia), (myocarditis))

Kawasaki Disease Outlook

With treatment, most kids with Kawasaki disease can avoid heart disease and death. Coronary artery aneurysms impact just 6% of kids who've been treated for the condition
