Health Check-up Tips

  • Schedule Regular Appointments
    • Visit your doctor at least once a year.
    • Keep track of your last check-up dates.
  • Monitor Vital Signs
    • Check blood pressure regularly.
    • Keep an eye on cholesterol and sugar levels.
  • Get Screenings and Tests
    • Undergo age-appropriate screenings (e.g., mammograms, colonoscopies).
    • Do routine blood tests for overall health.
  • Maintain Vaccinations
    • Stay updated on flu shots and boosters.
    • Check for travel-specific vaccinations.
  • Discuss Lifestyle and Mental Health
    • Talk about diet, exercise, and stress management.
    • Seek mental health support if needed.
